Changes due to COVID-19 pandemic

8 April 2020

Given the current situation pertaining to the COVID-19 pandemic, we are postponing APIO 2020 to 15 - 16 August 2020. Also, it is not mandatory for participants to take the contest at an official contest site.

You can check our page regarding changes due to COVID-19 pandemic to see the complete email sent to the delegation leaders containing the reason behind the decision as well. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact info.apio2020@toki.id.

APIO 2020 Onsite Cancellation

15 March 2020

Given the current situation pertaining to the COVID-19 outbreak, we regret to inform you that there will be no onsite contest option for APIO 2020. We also have removed any information related to the onsite contest from the website.

Having said that, Indonesia is still on track to host the contest with the online format that we used to have. We will keep you updated if there is any changes. If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact info.apio2020@toki.id.
